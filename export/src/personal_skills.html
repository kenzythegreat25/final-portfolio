Core

Six skills, repeated daily.

Not exhaustive. These are the muscles I've actually used to ship work for clients across four years of full-time inbox management.

01
Consultative outreach
Pull, don't push. Pain-first conversations that get prospects to qualify themselves before the call.
02
Inbox triage at volume
Hundreds of replies a day, sorted by intent in minutes. Hot leads up, noise out, nothing slips.
03
Voice & tone matching
I write replies indistinguishable from the founder's. Picky about commas, picky about caps.
04
Deliverability instinct
DNS, warmup, sender reputation. I can smell a soft bounce before the dashboard catches it.
05
Lead qualification
Building lists that don't waste sequences. Apollo / SN / Listkit, then human filters on top.
06
Calm under volume
Multi-client weeks, three time zones, no dropped balls. The least-flashy, most-valuable skill I have.

A typical day

Four hours, one rhythm.

07:00 — 08:00

Triage

Scan every active inbox. Tag intent, archive noise, surface anything hot for the founder.

08:00 — 10:00

Reply

Hand-write responses to qualified leads. Founder voice, pain-first framing, never templated.

10:00 — 11:30

Build

Lists, sequences, A/B variants. Whatever the campaign needs to keep the top of funnel honest.

11:30 — 12:00

Report

Short, useful summary to the founder. Replies, booked calls, anything weird in deliverability.
